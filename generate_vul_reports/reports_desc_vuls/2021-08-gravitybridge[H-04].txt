Type of vulnerability: Denial of Service (DoS)
Cause of vulnerability: Excessive data size from large validator sets or rapid validator updates causing log retrieval errors
Execution process: The `find_latest_valset` function attempts to retrieve validator set update events over a 5000 block range, but if the logs exceed 10mb due to a large number of validators or frequent updates, it results in an error, freezing the bridge and preventing attestations.
Vulnerable code: `let mut all_valset_events = web3.check_for_events(end_search.clone(), Some(current_block.clone()), vec![gravity_contract_address], vec![VALSET_UPDATED_EVENT_SIG],).await?;`
