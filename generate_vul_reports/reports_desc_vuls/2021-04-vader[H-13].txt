Type of vulnerability: Improper Input Validation
Cause of vulnerability: The `mintSynth` function does not validate if the `base` parameter is one of the authorized base tokens, allowing an attacker to manipulate the `base` parameter to a non-authorized token.
Execution process: An attacker transfers tokens to the pool and sets `base` to the transferred token, then calls `mintSynth(token, token, member)`. This leads to incorrect calculations in the swap output and breaks the pool's accounting, potentially inflating the amount of synths minted.
Vulnerable code: `mintSynth(token, token, member)` and related calculations in `_actualInput = getAddedAmount(base, token);` and `calcSwapOutput(_actualInput=tokenInput, mapToken_baseAmount[token], mapToken_tokenAmount[token]);`
