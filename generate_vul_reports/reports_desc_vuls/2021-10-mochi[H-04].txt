Type of vulnerability: Uncontrolled Asset Registration
Cause of vulnerability: Lack of validation in the `registerAsset()` function allows overwriting the `_assetClass` value of an already registered asset.
Execution process: An attacker can call the `registerAsset()` function to reset the `_assetClass` of an existing asset to `AssetClass.Sigma`, altering critical parameters such as `liquidationFactor()`, `riskFactor()`, `maxCollateralFactor()`, `liquidationFee()`, `keeperFee()`, and `maxFee()`, which can lead to unintended liquidations.
Vulnerable code: `registerAsset()` function in MochiProfileV0.sol without the `require(_assetClass[_asset] == 0, "Already exists");` check.
