Type of contract: Adapter for DyDx and Aave v2
Purpose of contract: To enable Set Protocol to interact with DyDx and Aave v2 lending protocols for shorting and longing positions.
Working process: The contract facilitates borrowing and lending of assets from DeFi protocols by wrapping the interactions in a standard interface that can be invoked by Set Protocol. It operates by executing trades that involve invoking specific actions on the underlying protocols, such as borrowing or repaying assets.
