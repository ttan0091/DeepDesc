Type of the contract: ERC20 Token
Purpose of the contract: To allow users to create, mine, and trade M2R and M2DAO tokens on xDai network using Nominex platform.
Working process of the contract: The contract tracks the total supply and balances of M2R and M2DAO tokens, allowing users to transfer tokens between accounts and approve other accounts to transfer on their behalf. Additionally, the contract contains an internal function to calculate the total value of different tokens in PEG, which is used in trading functions.
