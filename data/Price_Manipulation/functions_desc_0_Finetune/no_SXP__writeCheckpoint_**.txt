Type of contract: ERC20 + Proposal & Voting
        Key purpose of the contract: Allow users to stake tokens in order to create, support, or reject proposals, and to vote on proposals within a specific governance proposal/poll
        Highlevel overview of its operation: Users can stake their tokens, which are then counted as voting rights in the governance mechanism. The contract keeps track of the user's voting power and allows them to delegate their votes if desired. When proposals are created, votes determine the outcome of the proposal. 
        Specific security features implemented in the contract: Prevents overwriting previously saved checkpoints to protect voting history.
        The vulnerability or issue you need help with: Understanding the purpose and details of the checkpoints variable and the safe32 function in the context of voting power tracking.
