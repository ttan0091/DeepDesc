Type of contract: AMM (Automated Market Maker)
Purpose of the code: To calculate the liquidity value in USDC using the 2 tokens involved and their percentage of shares in the pool.
Working process: The core functionality involves the calculations based on the total supply of shares and the token balances in the pairs to determine the number of shares a user can redeem for a certain amount of USDC. This is achieved through the quote and quote_u functions.
Additional info: The contract stores the shares of each LP (Liquidity Provider) using the balanceOf function, and you can deposit and withdraw liquidity using the deposit and withdraw functions provided.
