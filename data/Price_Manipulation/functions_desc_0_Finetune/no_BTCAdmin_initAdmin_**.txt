Type of code: Ethereum Solidity Smart Contract
Name of the contract: MultiSignature
Purpose of the contract: A multisig contract designed for executing actions on behalf of an organization by requiring multiple approvals from authorized members.
Explanation of the code: The initAdmin function is called during the deployment of the MultiSignature contract. It sets up the initial configuration for the multisignature wallet, including the number of required signatures for various operations. In this case, it sets the ownerRequireNum and operatorRequireNum to 2, indicating that future actions will require 2 out of 3 owners to reach a consensus.
