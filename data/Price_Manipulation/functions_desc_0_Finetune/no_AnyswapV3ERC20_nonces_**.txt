Type of contract: ERC20 wrapper
Purpose of contract: To allow the locking and unlocking of specified ERC20 tokens to mint and burn corresponding ERC20 wrappers.
Key functions and their usage:
1. deposit: Locks specified ERC20 tokens and mints the corresponding ERC20 wrapper.
2. withdraw: Burns ERC20 wrapper tokens and unlocks the corresponding ERC20 tokens.
3. domint: Mints ERC20 wrapper tokens for a specified user.
4. burn: Burns ERC20 wrapper tokens for a specified user.
