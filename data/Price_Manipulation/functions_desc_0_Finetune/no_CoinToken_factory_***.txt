Type of smart contract: UniswapV2Router02
Working process: The UniswapV2Router02 contract facilitates various functions related to token pair management and liquidity provision on the Uniswap decentralized exchange (DEX). This includes functions for adding liquidity, removing liquidity, swapping tokens, and making tokentoETH or ETHtotoken swaps. Additionally, it supports feeontransfer token (FOTT) mechanisms to handle implicit fees.
