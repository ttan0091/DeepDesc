Type of contract: ERC20 Extension
Primary purpose: Melon token rebasing logic
Key functions: increaseApproval, decreaseApproval, rescuer, decreaseApprovalByCondition amplToTotalSupplyPeg, pegAdjustedTotalSupply
Working process: The contract allows for pausing and unpausing of token transfers, controlling approvals of token holders, and adjusting precision to support rebasing operations for deflationary tokens like Melon.
