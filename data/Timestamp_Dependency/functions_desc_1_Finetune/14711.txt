Type of contract: Crowdsale Contract
Purpose of the contract: To manage the sale of VERNAM tokens to the public
Execution flow: The contract sets the start time for the crowdsale and defines two additional periods - a 3-hour "Three Hot Hours" period and an 8-day "First Stage" period. The timings of these periods are calculated based on the current block timestamp. Throughout these periods, the contract allows for different minimum contributions and token bonuses to incentivize early investors.
