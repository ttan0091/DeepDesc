Type of contract: FoundingTeamLockup
Purpose of the contract: This contract locks up a portion of the total token supply for the founding team to be released gradually over a certain period for incentivizing long-term commitment.
Working process: The founding team can call the `unfreezeFoundingTeamBalance` function to release a certain percentage of the locked tokens after the initial cliff period. The function ensures that the current time is after the cliff time to prevent early withdrawals.
