Type of contract: Crowdsale
Purpose: to manage the distribution of bonuses to private sale investors
Key Variables and Functions:
- `bonusDeliverTime`: the timestamp after which private sale investors can claim their bonus
- `bonusUnsold`: the amount of unsold bonus peg tokens
- `returnBonus()`: a function that only becomes active after `bonusDeliverTime` and allows private sale investors to claim their bonus
