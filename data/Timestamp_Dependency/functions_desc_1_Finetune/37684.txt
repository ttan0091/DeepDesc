Type of contract: Lottery Smart Contract
Purpose: To provide a decentralized and fair platform for users to participate in a lottery that chooses a winner based on a pre-defined participation number.
Key features:
1. Only the contract creator can determine the winning number and winner.
2. Users participate by transferring a specific token to the contract.
3. The contract receives ETH during the PARTICIPATION phase but not during CHOOSE_WINNER.
4. Users can cancel their entries if the winner is not determined within a specified time after the participation period ends.
