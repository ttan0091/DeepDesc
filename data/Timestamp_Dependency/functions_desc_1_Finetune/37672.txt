Type of contract: Crowdsale
Purpose: To raise funds by selling tokens and to establish a grace period if the soft cap is reached.
Working process: Investors can purchase tokens by sending Ether to the Crowdsale contract. If the total funds raised (weiRaised) exceeds or equals the soft cap, and the grace period has not yet been initiated, the gracePeriodStart is set to the current block timestamp. Investors can then claim refunds during this grace period if the hard cap is not reached.
