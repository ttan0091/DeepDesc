Type of the contract: PGO 2 Year Vesting Contract
Primary function of the contract: Distribute vested tokens over a 2-year period with a 21-day offset
Key variables and their purpose: 
- cliff: Timestamp marking the start of the vesting period
- vested: Total number of vested tokens at a specific time
- timme: Current block timestamp
- time: Elapsed time since the start of the vesting period
- elapsedOffsets: Number of 21-day intervals that have passed since the start of the vesting period
