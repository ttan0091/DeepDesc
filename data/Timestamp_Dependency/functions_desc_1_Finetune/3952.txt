Type of contract: HorseFutures
Purpose: This contract serves as a marketplace where users can create and sell offers for future horse racing events. The offers specify the amount, price, and details of the race and the horse to be bet on.
Functionality: Users can create sell offers using the `sellOffer` function, which generates a unique ID for the offer based on its parameters. Once an offer is created, other users can call the `buyOffer` function to purchase tokens corresponding to the offer's details, pending the outcome of the race.
