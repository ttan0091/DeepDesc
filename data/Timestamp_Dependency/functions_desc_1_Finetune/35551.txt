Type of contract: Token Vesting Contract
Role of the contract: Manage the distribution of tokens over a set period, with a cliff and a vesting schedule, to a specific address that the token holder can claim over time.
Working process: The contract locks a specific amount of tokens for an address, which can be scheduled to be released over a specified vesting period, with an initial cliff. The token holder can use a specific function to claim a proportionate amount of tokens periodically after the initial cliff, until the full amount is claimed.
