Type of the contract: Incentive Pool (Reward Distribution)
    Purpose of the contract: The `ShortingRewards` contract is designed to manage and distribute rewards to users who provide liquidity to the project.
    Key functions of the contract: The `notifyRewardAmount` function is crucial as it determines the reward rate for users based on the rewards pool and the duration for which the rewards are active. Additionally, the `periodFinishUpdated` function ensures that the `periodFinish` variable is correctly set when sufficient rewards are added after the previous end period.
