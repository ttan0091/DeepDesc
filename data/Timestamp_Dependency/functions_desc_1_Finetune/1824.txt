Type of contract: Stablecoin or Stableswap
A stablecoin contract, ZMBC
Purpose of the contract: To provide a mechanism for market makers to settle their debts
Operation of the contract: The contract has a manager() function that allows the owner to call functions specific to sell-side or buy-side contracts.
Key attributes of the contract: The contract manages a debt exchange system using Zio Formal Debt Instruments to govern when to call them off.
