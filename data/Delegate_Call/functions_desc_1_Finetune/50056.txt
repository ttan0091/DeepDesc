Type of contract: Proxy contract
Initialization process: The MarketMaker contract is created and initialized by the initializer function in ECOProxy contract during deployment of ECOSafeMarketMakerTemplate template. The EllipMarketMakerLib#initialize function is called with the initialization parameters, where the EllipseMarketMaker is set on the EllipseMarket. The proxy contract is then linked to the new EllipseMarketMaker using ecp_owner, amm_owner and market_maker_owner with signature setOwners(address ecp_owner,address amm_owner, address market_maker_owner).
