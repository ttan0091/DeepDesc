Type of contract: ERC20
Primary purpose: To enable and track the ability of an address to participate in a liquid staking protocol.
Mechanism: Staking the entire balance of the tracker token in the GovTrudy contract for a variable time period (tMin), receiving staked tracker tokens, ownership tokens NFTs, and an exit fee from the GovTrudy contract, while still being able to transfer and receive the tracker token from elsewhere, subject to increased state fees on outgoing transfers if done too close to timeunlocking governable timer.
