Type of code: Ethereum (Solidity smart contract) 
Programming language: Solidity
Purpose of the code: ERC20like functionality suitable for Rogan.network's collections
Working process: The code integrates into the Rogan.network's collections system, allowing NFT owners to mint specific Tokens that are then bridgesupported with growth Engines like Solarization Engine and AI Staking Engine. 
The NFT owners can permit Vault to transfer specific Tokens on their behalf using the permit function, which is similar to ERC20 permit function.
