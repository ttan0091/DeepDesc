Type of smart contract: DelegatorVoting Compound Token (Uni)
Role of the smart contract: Facilitate zero gas fee delegation service with precise earnings distribution based on historical token balances
Summary of the working process: Users stake Uni tokens in the contract, which mints an equivalent amount of sUni tokens. These sUni tokens represent staked Uni tokens and accrue interest from Compound. Users can delegate their votes to an account at zero gas cost. The minter can mint Uni tokens and receive accrued interest, which are proportionally distributed to the delegated votes.
