Type of contract: ERC20 Token
Role of the contract: Multiplicative Denomination Common Token (MDCT) with a unique supply model
Coding language: Solidity
Cryptographic algorithms used: SHA256 (blockchainaggregated)
Intended functionality: The contract is designed to act as a common unit of account and a means for transferring value among different blockchain networks, specifically for the inbound and outbound liquidations and highway networks. The unique supply model ensures the pegging institutions cannot inflate or deflate the currency supply.
