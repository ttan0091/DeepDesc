Type of the contract: Vesting Contract
        Purpose: To manage the vesting of DAO tokens for participants
        Key Functions: 
        1. Initialization: To set the tokens and staff that will receive them
        2. Distribution: To release vested tokens to participants
        3. Emergency Withdraw: To allow owner to withdraw remaining tokens after the end of vesting schedule
        4. Snapshotted Mint: To mint new tokens for certain accounts and maintain snapshot balances for voting purposes
