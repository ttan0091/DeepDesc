Type of the system: Automated Market Maker (AMM)
Purpose of the system: Allow users to buy/sell tokens without the need for an order book or counterparty
Basic overview of the system's function: Users can swap tokens for other tokens or ETH using liquidity pools
Role of liquidity providers: Provide liquidity by depositing pairs of tokens into liquidity pools, earning fees and tokens in return
Mechanisms for preventing arbitrage opportunities: Slow movement of spot prices using an automatic rebalancing mechanism in the pools
