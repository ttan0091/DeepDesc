Type of contract: ERC20based AMM (Automated Market Maker)
Primary function: To provide liquidity for a pool that consists of 1e18 FLOAT/FW tokens and 1e18 peg tokens, i.e., equal value of liquidity.
Working process: Users can add liquidity using either ERC20 pegs or ether via WETH. LP tokens are minted to represent the share of the pool the depositor owns.
