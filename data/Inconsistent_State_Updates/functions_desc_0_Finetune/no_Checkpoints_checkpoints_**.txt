Type of the software: Ethereum Smart Contract
Purpose of the software: Managing a token’s voting weight and automating votes by delegating voting power to a strategy contract
Working process of the software: The leverage and deleverage functions are called by an external strategy contract, which temporarily locks collateral through the PegPool and automatically votes the locked’s owner's voting power. This process reduces oracle and voting fees without the need for forced voting, while also providing the LPs with a lemonstomarily adjusted to be overcollateralized.
