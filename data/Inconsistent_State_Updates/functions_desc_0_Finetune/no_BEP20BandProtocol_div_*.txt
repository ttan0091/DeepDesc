Type of the system: Controller Contract
Purpose of the system: To calculate the Dollar total value of underlying collateral in USD based on its individual stablecoin market rate.
Working process: The controller contract fetches the stablecoin total supply and controller balance from each strategy using the getPriceOrRerate and balanceOf functions, calculates the platform fee, and then calls the getStrategiesRange function to calculate the percentage amount of the deposit dollar total value from all strategies.
