Type of contract: Transparent Liquid Democracy (TLD) via Politicoin
        Purpose of the contract: Implement and enact policies through direct voting and delegation
        Key functionalities: Proposing and voting on policies, delegating votes, enacting approved proposals
        Components of the contract: PoliciesRegister, PolicyQueue, PolicyUpgrades, Proposals, Participants
        Mechanism of the contract: Allows participants to create policies, vote on them, and delegate votes to other participants; Policies that meet the vote threshold are enacted and can trigger various actions such as transferring funds or calling external functions
