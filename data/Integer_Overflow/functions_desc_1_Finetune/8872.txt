Task: Explain the working process of the `ELTWagerLedger` contract, specifically the `withdraw` function, using the code provided.

1. The `ELTWagerLedger` contract stores the commitments of users who have locked their funds in the ELT protocol.
2. The `withdraw` function allows users to withdraw their funds from the protocol.
3. When a user withdraws funds, the corresponding commitments are reduced, and the withdrawn funds are transferred back to the user's address.
