Type of the contract: Decentralized Exchange (DEX)
Events used in the contract: BalancesUpdated 
How the contract works: Users can deposit and withdraw ERC20 tokens, and trade them on a DEX platform. The `EthertoToken` and `TokentoEther` functions facilitate token swaps. The `sumTokensInPeg` function calculates the worth of the user's combined tokens in terms of ether.
