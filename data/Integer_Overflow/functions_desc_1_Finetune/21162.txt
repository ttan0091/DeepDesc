Type of the contract: Prediction Market
        Purpose of the contract: To allow users to predict whether the price of Ethereum will be higher or lower after 5 minutes, using the `cleanupAbandonedGame` function to calculate the elapsed time since the game started.
        Detailed explanation of the `cleanupAbandonedGame` function: This function calculates the time elapsed since the contract deployment year (2021) until the current block's timestamp. The purpose is to determine if a game has been abandoned for more than 12 hours, at which point the game is declared a draw and can be cleaned up.
