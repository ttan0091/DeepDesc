Type of contract: Crowdsale and Token Generation
Creator/Finality: Immutable, creator (founder) can call `buyRecipient()` to add tokens to any address
Source of Funds: Initial ETH from founder, ongoing ETH from transactions
Token Distribution: Token allocation is not tied to ETH sent, controlled by founder
Approval Mechanism: No direct approval, implicit approval through the ability of the founder to add tokens to any address
