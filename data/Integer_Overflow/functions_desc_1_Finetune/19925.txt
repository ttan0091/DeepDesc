Type of contract: ERC223 Token Compatible
Language/Version: Solidity 0.8.10
Description: The contract is designed to work with ERC223 and ERC677 token transfers by extending ERC20. It allows for additional data to be attached to transfers, a feature not available in traditional ERC20 transfers.
Objective: To consolidate small balances of different tokens into a single Peg balance for more efficient and cost-effective withdrawals.
