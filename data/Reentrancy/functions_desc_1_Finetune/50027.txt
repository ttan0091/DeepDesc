Type of the contract: Non-Custodial Holder Contract
Role of the contract: Acts as an escrow, receiving and holding collateral assets for a certain period before releasing them
Working process: The contract receives Solana and Ethereum assets, verifies their amount and pegs them to their USD value, holds the assets for a predetermined period, and then releases them to the owner if no hack is claimed. If a hack is claimed, the assets are released to the vault.
