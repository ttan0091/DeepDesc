Type of the contract: Escrow Contract
Purpose of the contract: To facilitate the exchange of cryptocurrency for ERC20 tokens through a multi-step process involving liquidity provision and token swaps.
Working process of the contract: The contract provides functions for creating and closing orders, depositing and withdrawing ERC20 tokens, and executing orders through token swaps. It employs the `InvariantCheckerPool` to ensure that the pool's underlying ERC20 tokens do not deviate from an initial fixed supply.
