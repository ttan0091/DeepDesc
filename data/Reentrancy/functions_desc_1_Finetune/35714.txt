Type of contract: Crowdpooling Vault
Purpose of the contract: To facilitate the launch of a new project with a crowdpooling mechanism, where users deposit fungible tokens to receive a share of the new project's token.
Functionality: The contract allows users to deposit multiple types of fungible tokens, which are then converted into the desired token and LP tokens, proportional to their value. Users can also provide LP tokens directly. Additionally, users can redeem their shares for the underlying tokens or sell operations fees.
