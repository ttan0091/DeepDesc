Type of contract: Private sale/Swapping of stablecoin directly with farm
Primary function: Creating liquidity pool positions for stablecoinA and stablecoinB
Working process: The contract converts stablecoinA to pegToken by swapping the token from stabelcoinA to pegToken and then from pegToken to stabelcoinB. The pegToken is a stable value token, which is determined by `Chainlink` or `Binance` reliable reference data.
