Type of contract: Crypto
Purpose of contract: To provide a universal payment solution especially for new and small businesses.
Core functionality: The contract provides the ability to accept various forms of payments by integrating payment methods using plugins (the processPayment function). This allows merchants to easily receive payments in a wide range of assets while the fee structure supports fees for both paying and receiving transactions. Additionally, the contract has a refund system for disaster handling and a default watchlist for blacklisted addresses.
