Type of the contract: Automated Market Maker (AMM) Vault Contract
Mechanism of operation: The AMM Vault Contract provides various functions to manage and interact with Liquidity Provider (LP) tokens. Users can add, remove, and migrate liquidity using these functions. Additionally, the contract includes functions to sweep zeroaddress residual amounts and to claim rewards and tokens from the underlying AMM WSwap contract.
Key functions in the contract: 
1. removeLiquidity
2. removeLiquidityETH
3. migrate
4. collectRewards
5. claimAndCollect
6. distributeRewards
7. nftMigration
