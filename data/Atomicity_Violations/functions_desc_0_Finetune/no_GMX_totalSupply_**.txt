Type of the contract: ERC20
Working process: 
1. Initial supply of tokens (433,000 MUBY tokens) is minted to the creator's address on deployment.
2. The minted tokens can be transferred from the creator's address to other addresses using the transfer function of the ERC20 standard.
3. Users can check their token balance using the balanceOf function.
4. The total supply of tokens in circulation can be obtained using the totalSupply function.
