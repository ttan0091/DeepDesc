Type of code: Automated Market Making (AMM) Token Model
Purpose of the code: To facilitate market making and liquidity provision for token pairs
Working process: The removeLiquidity function in the AMM model is called when a market maker removes liquidity. It returns the amounts of assets and commitment states (fCash) corresponding to the shares (LPTokens) burned. The function calculates the amounts of asset and commitment state tokens by calling the MarketState.removeLiquidity function.
