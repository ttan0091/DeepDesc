Type of contract: SolvencyPortfolio
Purpose of the contract: Managing the portfolio of a prefunded cashsettled perpetual contract
Working process:
1. Users deposit liquidity assets and use them as collateral.
2. The contract sells a portion of the liquidity pool to set up the perpetual markets and accrue the additional liquidity in the contract.
3. As assets become underwater, the contract is able to withdraw liquidity tokens and settlement funds to manage them.
4. This function removes the required number of liquidity tokens to make the portfolio solvent again.
